A Very Vaccuum Birthday

A Very Vaccuum Birthday from Naila N on Vimeo.


A Very Vaccuum Birthday was our first attempt at making an animation in toonboom. We gave ourselves a week to write the script, storyboard and ship a fully animated video. We used Fiver for the voice-over work. Unfortunately, we did not get to the ink and colour before the deadline. Co-created with Dylan Harness.
